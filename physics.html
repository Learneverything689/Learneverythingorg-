Introduction to Physics

Physics is the natural science that studies the fundamental principles governing the behavior of matter and energy in the universe. It encompasses a wide range of phenomena, from the smallest subatomic particles to the largest galaxies, and everything in between.

Branches of Physics

  • Classical Mechanics
  • Thermodynamics
  • Electromagnetism
  • Optics
  • Quantum Mechanics
  • Particle Physics

Key Concepts in Physics

  • Motion and Forces
  • Energy and Work
  • Electricity and Magnetism
  • Waves and Vibrations
  • Quantum Theory
  • Relativity

Physics plays a central role in understanding how the universe works, from the behavior of atoms to the motion of celestial bodies. It underpins many other scientific disciplines and is essential for technological advancements in fields such as engineering, astronomy, and telecommunications.
