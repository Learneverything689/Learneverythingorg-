Introduction to Biology

Biology is the scientific study of living organisms and their interactions with each other and their environment. It is a diverse and vast field that encompasses various sub-disciplines, ranging from molecular biology to ecology.

Branches of Biology

  • Botany
  • Zoology
  • Microbiology
  • Genetics
  • Ecology
  • Cell Biology

Key Concepts in Biology

  • Cell Theory
  • Evolution
  • Genetics and Heredity
  • Ecosystems and Biodiversity
  • Human Anatomy and Physiology
  • Developmental Biology

Studying biology helps us understand the complexities of life, from the molecular mechanisms within cells to the interactions between different organisms in ecosystems. It also plays a crucial role in fields such as medicine, biotechnology, and environmental conservation.
