Introduction to Geology

Geology is the scientific study of the Earth, including its structure, composition, processes, and history. It examines the formation and changes of rocks, minerals, mountains, valleys, oceans, and other geological features that shape the planet.

Branches of Geology

  • Mineralogy
  • Petrology
  • Structural Geology
  • Stratigraphy
  • Geophysics
  • Paleontology

Key Concepts in Geology

  • Plate Tectonics
  • Rock Cycle
  • Volcanism and Earthquakes
  • Sedimentation
  • Erosion and Weathering
  • Geological Time Scale

Geology is essential for understanding Earth's history, natural resources, geological hazards, and the processes that continue to shape our planet. It plays a crucial role in fields such as mining, oil exploration, environmental assessment, and natural disaster mitigation.
